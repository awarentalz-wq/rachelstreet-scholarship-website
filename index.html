How Booking Works

Two ways to book

Every unit can be booked either way — pick what matters more to you.

Quicker

Airbnb

Book instantly through Airbnb using the links above. Airbnb adds its own service and booking fees on top of the nightly rate, so the total cost is higher — those fees aren't something we control.

Lower Cost

Direct Lease

  1. We send a $45 background check — usually approved within a few hours.
  2. You'll receive a BreezeDoc lease to sign (like DocuSign) and a TurboTenant invite to set up your account.
  3. In TurboTenant, add a payment method and pay first month's rent plus a refundable deposit equal to 50% of one month's rent, then set up autopay.
  4. After move-out, we inspect the unit — if it's in good condition and lease terms were followed, your deposit is refunded.
